# 06 — What CoachOS Actually Does (plain-language guide)

Every other document in this repo is written for the team — market data, architecture, build order. This one is written for **a coach reading it for the first time**, with no jargon. If a sentence in here needs explaining, it's written wrong — say so and we'll fix it.

A nicer, visual version of this same content lives at [`features.html`](../features.html) — open it in a browser.

---

## Never write a session summary from scratch again

After a session, jot down rough notes — even messy, half-finished ones. CoachOS turns them into a clean, warm summary email ready to send your client, plus a private action list just for you. **Nothing goes to your client until you've read it and hit approve.**

## Never lose a lead to a forgotten follow-up

Every enquiry lands on one simple board: *New → Talked → Proposal → Won.* If someone's gone quiet, CoachOS tells you and writes the follow-up message for you. You just read it and hit send.

## Write your newsletter without staring at a blank page

CoachOS learns how you write from a few samples of your own writing. Every week it drafts a social post and a newsletter in your voice — based on real (fully anonymized) themes from your actual sessions. It sends through your own subscriber list, tracks who opened it, and handles unsubscribes automatically. You edit if you want, you approve, it sends.

## Fill your calendar without the back-and-forth

Share one link. People pick a time that works for both of you. Reminders go out automatically so fewer people miss sessions — and if someone doesn't show up, CoachOS helps you get them rebooked with one click.

## Get contracts signed and get paid, without chasing anyone

Send a coaching agreement — your client signs it right on their phone, and it's legally binding. Invoices go out and get chased automatically if they're late. One glance shows you what's been paid and what hasn't.

## Put the boring repeat tasks on autopilot

Turn on ready-made routines — "if a lead goes quiet for 6 days, follow up," "if an invoice is 3 days late, send a reminder." You approve the exact wording once when you turn a routine on. After that, CoachOS handles the *timing* — it never invents new wording on its own.

## Get found by new clients

Your own public profile — your specialty, your rates, your story — listed where people search for a coach like you. Every enquiry from that listing drops straight into your Leads board, no extra work on your end. This is a listing, not a marketplace: **CoachOS never takes a cut of what you charge your clients.**

## See how your practice is really doing, at a glance

One dashboard: money coming in, sessions this week, deals in progress. CoachOS quietly notices when a client's engagement seems to be drifting — missed sessions, stalled goals — so you can reach out before they disappear, not after.

## Emails that come from you, not a robot

Connect your Gmail once. From then on, everything CoachOS sends on your behalf comes from your real email address — and when a client replies, it lands in your actual inbox, exactly like it would if you'd typed it yourself.

---

## The one promise that covers everything above

**CoachOS never sends anything — an email, a post, a message — without you seeing the exact words first and choosing to send them.** AI drafts. You decide. Every time, no exceptions. That's not a limitation we're planning to remove later — it's the whole reason coaches will trust this with their client relationships.

## What you're not getting (on purpose)

CoachOS doesn't coach your clients for you, doesn't replace the actual sessions, and doesn't make judgment calls about your clients. It handles the admin around your coaching so you have more time for the coaching itself.
