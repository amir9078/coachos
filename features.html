What CoachOS actually does for you

No jargon. Just what happens when you use it, day to day.

❧

Never write a session summary from scratch again

After a session, jot down rough notes — even messy, half-finished ones. CoachOS turns them into a clean, warm summary email ready to send your client, plus a private action list just for you.

Nothing sends until you approve it
⚘

Never lose a lead to a forgotten follow-up

Every enquiry lands on one simple board: New → Talked → Proposal → Won. If someone's gone quiet, CoachOS tells you and writes the follow-up message for you. You just read it and hit send.

✎

Write your newsletter without staring at a blank page

CoachOS learns how you write from a few samples of your own writing. Every week it drafts a social post and a newsletter in your voice, from real (anonymized) themes in your own sessions. It sends through your own subscriber list and tracks who opened it.

Powered by Listmonk — listmonk.app
◷

Fill your calendar without the back-and-forth

Share one link. People pick a time that works for both of you. Reminders go out automatically so fewer people miss sessions — and if someone doesn't show, CoachOS helps you get them rebooked with one click.

§

Get contracts signed and get paid, without chasing anyone

Send a coaching agreement — your client signs it right on their phone, legally binding. Invoices go out and get chased automatically if they're late. One glance shows you what's been paid and what hasn't.

⚙

Put the boring repeat tasks on autopilot

Turn on ready-made routines — "if a lead goes quiet for 6 days, follow up," "if an invoice is 3 days late, send a reminder." You approve the exact wording once, when you turn a routine on. After that, CoachOS handles the timing — it never invents new wording on its own.

☍

Get found by new clients

Your own public profile — your specialty, your rates, your story — listed where people search for a coach like you. Every enquiry from that listing drops straight into your Leads board, no extra work.

A listing, never a commission on what you charge
◈

See how your practice is really doing, at a glance

One dashboard: money coming in, sessions this week, deals in progress. CoachOS quietly notices when a client's engagement seems to be drifting, so you can reach out before they disappear, not after.

✉

Emails that come from you, not a robot

Connect your Gmail once. From then on, everything CoachOS sends on your behalf comes from your real email address — and when a client replies, it lands in your actual inbox, exactly like normal.

☀

Start your day already caught up

Open CoachOS and there's a short, honest briefing waiting: the top news in your field, a quick catch-up on what's happening in the world, and one thing worth learning today — five minutes, tops. Every headline links back to where it actually came from.

Readable on our website, even before you sign up
The one promise that covers everything above

CoachOS never sends anything without you seeing the exact words first.

An email, a post, a message — AI drafts, you decide. Every time, no exceptions. That's not a limitation we're planning to remove later. It's the whole reason coaches will trust this with their client relationships.

What you're not getting (on purpose)

CoachOS doesn't coach your clients for you, doesn't replace the actual sessions, and doesn't make judgment calls about your clients. It handles the admin around your coaching so you have more time for the coaching itself.
