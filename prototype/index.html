    The line we never cross

    AI runs the desk. Humans do the humaning.

    CoachOS never coaches your clients and never sends a word without your approval. That's a product principle and our EU AI Act posture in one.

    The platform does

    • Turn rough session notes into client-ready summaries
    • Brief you before every session on commitments & blockers
    • Score leads, spot silence, and draft the follow-up
    • Draft posts & newsletters in your voice
    • Chase invoices, send reminders, recover no-shows
    • Flag at-risk clients before they churn

    Humans still do

    • The coaching itself — every session, every relationship
    • Approve every message before it leaves the building
    • Onboarding & migration — done with you, by our team
    • Done-for-you marketing — human editors, AI-assisted
    • The community — coaches referring coaches
    • Judgment. Always.
    Build order · gated phases

    Wedge first. Empire later, if earned.

    Each phase ships only when the previous one passes a hard gate — the discipline that separates this plan from the all-in-one graveyard.

    1

    The wedge — Client delivery

    Notes→summaries, prep briefs, timeline, scheduling embed. Months 1–3.

    Gate: 5 pilots weekly · 3 paid
    2

    The money loop

    Bookings, contracts, e-sign, Stripe billing. Months 4–8.

    Gate: 25 paying · churn <5%
    3

    The growth loop

    Leads CRM, marketing composer, Automations, Directory, Daily Briefing, admin MCP tool. Months 9–14.

    Gate: 100 paying · service profitable
    4

    Intelligence + Community v1

    Practice dashboard, at-risk AI, and the Coach Community feed — connect, post, like, comment, share. Months 15–20.

    Gate: earned, not assumed
    5

    Community v2 + scale

    Direct messaging and real-time chat — a deliberately separate build from the feed. Then corporate/L&D, from leverage. Months 21+.

    Gate: v1 shows real engagement first
